Step-by-Step Tutorial

Steps 1-8: How to Draw Eyes

Step-by-step process:

  1. Step 1: Draw a basic circle for the eye foundation
  2. Step 2: Add a smaller circle inside - this will be the iris
  3. Step 3: Draw the pupil in the center of the iris
  4. Step 4: Add a white highlight in the upper part of the pupil
  5. Step 5: Outline the eye contour with black color
  6. Step 6: Add shadows and reflections for volume
  7. Step 7: Experiment with iris colors
  8. Step 8: Final touches and details

Detailing and Style

At the second stage, focus on details. Add shadows, reflections, textures. Experiment with colors - turquoise is the primary color of Irys, but don't be afraid to add contrasting elements to create depth.
